Our Mission

Our work is to produce new insights into device-edge-cloud data-driven communicating frameworks and future transportation services, build digital replicas of real-world entities based on AI, big data, cloud/edge computing, and mixed reality. Our goal aims to create an environment with respectfulness, courtesy, equality, inclusion to explore thoughts and bring standards of excellence to what we do as reseachers.
