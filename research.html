Apr. 13, 2023

Our two papers, "Multi-View Multi-Scale Driver Action Recognition with Vision Transformer" and "Peer-to-Peer Federated Continual Learning for Naturalistic Driving Action Recognition," have been accepted to 2023 IEEE/CVF Computer Vision and Pattern Recognition Conference (CVPR) Workshops, which will be held in Vancouver, Canada.

Our two papers "ViT-DD: Multi-Task Vision Transformer for Semi-Supervised Driver Distraction Detection" and "Driver Monitoring-Based Lane-Change Prediction: A Personalized Federated Learning Framework" have been accepted to 2023 IEEE Intelligent Vehicles Symposium in Anchorage, AK.
